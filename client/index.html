“Therefore what God has joined together, let no one separate." Mark 10:9

OUR  STORY

Corey and Erika met in 2006, during Erika's first week at Michigan State University – kicking off Corey’s second year as a Spartan.

The two quickly developed a strong friendship, affectionately nicknaming each other "Fav", short for Favorite. As their friendship flourished, their bond became evident to those closest to them, as they began to notice what Corey and Erika would soon find out . . . they were meant to be.

On March 14, 2009 the two officially began dating, an adventure that would lead them from East Lansing MI, to Orlando, FL once they both graduated from MSU. The two returned to their home in Detroit, MI in September 2013. Corey asked Erika to marry him on May 30, 2016 at an intimate dinner with friends in Saint Clair Michigan. Shortly after, the two decided to start off their new chapter in Los Angeles, California, where they currently reside.
